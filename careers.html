Current Vacancies

EDITORIAL INTERNSHIP

Avenue 32 is looking for an Editorial Intern. This internship role will include helping our Fashion & Editorial Assistant to prepare for shoots, liaise with PR companies and efficiently complete pulls and returns. It will also include assisting the Editorial Team with the creation of content for our Social Media platforms, designer bio's and fashion features along with general administrative duties. Applicants must be proactive, organized, detail-oriented, creative, professional, and have a positive attitude. The successful candidates will need to have a strong interest in fashion or a fashion-related degree, have accomplished written English skills, and great organisational skills. Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and InDesign would be beneficial and strong IT skills are essential. Above all, candidates need to be hardworking, enthusiastic and have a great eye for detail; they must be able to handle multiple tasks at once and work well under pressure. Applications including a cover letter and CV should be sent to careers@avenue32.com with your name and the role you are applying for in the subject line.

CLIENT SERVICES INTERNSHIP

Avenue 32 is looking for a full-time, fixed-term Client Services Intern to be based at our West London Head Office. This role sits at the centre of the organisation as a high-profile, customer-facing liaison between the Buying Team; Logistics; Client Development and Customer Services.

Responsibilities

  • Assisting the Client Services team with general tasks and admin support.
  • Working on the order system to process customer orders.
  • 'Walking’ Customer orders through to completion.
  • Providing crucial style and outfit co-ordination advice to customers.
  • Liaising with the warehouse team and courier companies.
  • Facilitating customer returns.

Skills and Experience

  • Excellent communication skills and strong command of English.
  • IT competency: particularly Excel, Outlook & PowerPoint.
  • Excellent written skills.
  • Able to work under pressure.
  • Personable, approachable and articulate.
  • Flexibility and resourcefulness as part of a small, interdependent and growing team.
  • Interest in and strong knowledge about luxury Womenswear.
  • A natural dedication to service excellence.

Requirements

  • Must be available to work Monday - Friday between 9.30am - 6pm. (Flexible working hours options exist).

Applications including a cover letter and CV should be sent to careers@avenue32.com with your name and the role you are applying for in the subject line.
