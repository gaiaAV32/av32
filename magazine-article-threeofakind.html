Is the term ‘It bag’ redundant? We are unconvinced. Amey Martin, Les Petits Joueurs and Sonia Rykiel are three luxury bag brands ticking all the right boxes in extremely desirable ways. Beyond their essential-carrying functionality, which is always a given; they’re all made from the finest materials with equal importance placed on craftsmanship and design. So far so chic but their details definitely have the insta-fab factor too. The contrasting flap and slim, hardware strap of Amey Martin’s simple shoulder bags should delight minimalists and colour lovers alike; while the surprisingly versatile rainbow of lego, stripes, beads and snake inlays decorating Les Petits Joueurs’ ladylike styles will see you through to dawn from dusk and back again. For cooler, roomier shapes dripping in gemstones, quilted stripes and fringing; look no further than Sonia Rykiel, the iconic French house that’s synonymous with irreverent 70s glamour.
