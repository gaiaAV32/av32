2015 has been a big one for Mr. Alexander Wang. It marks the 10th anniversary of his brand, and the year he chose to open his first UK store. Back in May Miley Cyrus and Zoe Kravitz wowed at the MET Gala in his studded dresses from AW15. Wang's runway flatforms, from the same collection, were snapped up after the show by everyone from Madonna to Rihanna. And 2015 is the year that Alexander Wang announced his departure as creative director of Balenciaga to focus on his namesake label And whether you commemorate the occasion with a signature accessory or ar crucial RTW piece: there will be no better moment to capture fashion history in the making.
