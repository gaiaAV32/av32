Bows and knots are essentially the same thing but the former gets all the pretty plaudits. The only real difference is that a bow decoratively reveals rather than conceals. Or maybe the knot gets knocked because it’s so comfortable in its faultless functionality, and basic beauty just doesn’t need such overt ostentation? The knot, perhaps the most down-to-earth of details, is a daily occurrence – safely and stylishly securing the strands of modern life. Traditionally used for ties and rope, the knot is hitting hemlines and wrapping waists and wrists this season with minimal fuss, but we expect nothing less of this composure-keeping accoutrement.
