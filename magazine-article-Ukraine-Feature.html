Country Retreat

All of these styles are smartly casual and super versatile in their own right, even those tan mules, so they’ll work wonders at social gatherings when you don’t want to try too hard.

The KNIT

Jonathan Saunders’ ‘it’ knit is patchworked to perfection. Triple toned and textured, the diagonal hem artfully mirrors the statement stripes. The modern minimalism it exudes has wear-anywhere appeal.

The SKIRT

Pleated skirts are still ultra-popular but Leur Logette’s has the most feminine flair. Besides the pink, lace and silk is folded side by side and the ribbon waist is ruffled for a girly-yet-grown up feel.

The COAT

Trenches don’t have to be beige and Litkovskaya’s lace version is the prettiest twist on the tradition. It can be a light layer, a delicate dress topper and much more.

The JUMPSUIT

Isa Arfen simplifies the chambray jumpsuit by bypassing buttons, removing sleeves and widening cropped legs. So far so chic but the belt, which looks best bowed, adds the brand’s witty charm.

The JACKET

The classic, collarless cut of Paskal’s jacket allows the surface circles to shine attracting admiring glances at every opportunity. They’re tempting to touch and sway playfully with every step.
